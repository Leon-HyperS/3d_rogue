# Art Direction

## Project

`Relic Run`

## Visual Goal

A small stylized 3D stealth game set in a ruined courtyard at dusk.

The player is a bright readable hero moving through a compact arena of stone walls, fences, rocks, and plants while avoiding monster patrols. The scene should feel playful and readable first, atmospheric second.

## Tone

- stylized, readable, game-like
- light tension rather than horror
- compact prototype scale
- clear silhouettes and interaction targets

## Camera

- elevated third-person / isometric-leaning follow camera
- wide enough to support stealth readability
- stable framing over cinematic motion

## Color and Lighting

- cool dusk background
- warm chest/key highlight colors
- readable enemy silhouettes
- bright player contrast against darker ground and walls

## Local Asset Basis

Primary packs:

- `Free_Assets/Platformer Game Kit - Dec 2021`
- `Free_Assets/Ultimate Monsters`

Curated runtime subset:

- player: `Character.gltf`
- enemies: `Orc.gltf`, `Demon.gltf`
- key: `Key.gltf`
- goal and interactions: `Chest.gltf`, `Door.gltf`, `Lever.gltf`
- environment props: fences, rocks, plants, rock platforms

## Readability Priorities

1. player and enemies must be easy to distinguish
2. key, lever, and chest must read as objective objects immediately
3. patrol areas should be visually understandable
4. walls and props should support navigation without cluttering the view
